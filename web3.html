🔍 What is Cryptocurrency?

   

      Cryptocurrency is a type of digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography       for security. It operates independently of central banks and governments.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

   
      Is crypto safe to use?      

Yes, but always use trusted platforms and secure your wallet.

   
   
      Do I need a bank to use crypto?      

No. You only need a crypto wallet and internet connection.

   
   
      Can I earn money with crypto?      

Yes, through trading, staking, NFTs, and more. But be cautious — it's volatile.
